Beef Chow Fun (乾炒牛河)

Serves: 2

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 20 minutes

This homemade Beef Chow Fun features thick, chewy rice noodles, tender steak, and crisp vegetables coated in a savory brown sauce. I cook these restaurant-style fried noodles in just 30 minutes, making it a satisfying main dish to serve my family.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • ½ lb beef flank steak
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch

Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons chicken broth
  • 1½ tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 1½ tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Stir Fry

  • 7 oz dried rice noodles (or 12 oz / 360 g fresh thick rice noodles)
  • 3 tablespoons peanut oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 1 thumb ginger, julienned
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • ¼ white onion, sliced
  • 4 loosely packed cups bean sprouts
  • 4 green onions, sliced to 2” (5 cm) pieces, white halved, white and green separated
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil

Directions

  1. Slice beef against the grain into ⅛” (3mm) thick and 2” (5 cm) long pieces. Add light soy sauce and Shaoxing wine, mix well, then add cornstarch and mix again. Let marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Combine all sauce ingredients and set aside.
  3. If using dried noodles, cook according to package instructions minus 1 minute. Rinse with cold water and drain. Toss with 1 tsp sesame oil to prevent sticking. If using fresh noodles, separate by hand.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large nonstick skillet over high heat. Spread beef in a single layer and cook until golden on the bottom (1–2 minutes). Flip and cook briefly, then transfer to a plate.
  5. In the same skillet, add remaining 2 tablespoons oil. Cook ginger, garlic, onion, and white part of green onion until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  6. Add noodles and toss to coat with oil. Add beef back in, pour in the sauce, and toss everything together.
  7. Add bean sprouts and continue tossing until they just start to soften (1–2 minutes). Add green onion parts and toss a few more times until sauce is absorbed. Serve immediately.
