Guide To Navigate In Conventions

Practical Tips + Important Convention Manners

✨ Practical Tips for Beginners

🚛 Use a Small Cart / Luggage Trolley

For big or complicated costumes, carry everything in a small push cart and change on site.

  • Wear inner clothes + shorts underneath
  • Avoid long toilet queues in full cosplay

🎀 Wig Comfort Hack

Place a folded tissue under the wig cap along the hairline to prevent discomfort.

👟 Footwear Tip

Bring comfortable slippers or flats if wearing high heels or any other footwear that could be tiring.

🧪 Pre-Convention Preparation

  • Try on the full costume at home
  • Test the wig in advance
  • Make a checklist so you don’t forget small accessories
  • Bring makeup touch-up kit (lipstick, powder, blush, setting spray, etc.)

🚫 Things You Should NOT Do at Comic Conventions

1. Don’t Harass or Bother Cosplayers

Avoid sudden proposals, grabbing, or dramatic scenes.

2. No Sneaky / Hidden Photos

Always ask for permission before taking any photo.

3. Don’t Take from Other Cosplayers Without Permission

Don’t suddenly take their props or items without asking first.

4. Don’t Make Weird Requests

Avoid awkward or inappropriate requests.

5. Respect Their Hard Work

Don’t dirty or mishandle their costume.

💖 Be kind and respectful. A simple “May I take a photo?” goes a long way!
