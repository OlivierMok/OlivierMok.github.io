About Cosplay Craft Academy

Hi, I'm Olivier!

I've been a cosplayer for 4 years and I decided to share my knowledge to everyone!

My Story

When I first started cosplaying, I was so excited but also super overwhelmed. My makeup looked muddy, my wigs were shiny and plastic-looking, and I had no idea what tools I actually needed. I wasted a lot of money and time learning through trial and error.

That's why I created Cosplay Craft Academy — to make learning cosplay easier, friendlier, and more fun for beginners. No gatekeeping, just practical tips from someone who's been there!

Our Mission

To help new cosplayers build confidence through clear, step-by-step tutorials on makeup, wig styling, tools, and character-specific techniques.

Whether you're doing your first budget cosplay or improving your skills, this site is made for you.

What You'll Find Here

  • Beginner-friendly makeup tutorials
  • Wig styling & trimming guides
  • Essential tools & kit recommendations
  • Character-specific breakdowns (Miku, Power, and more coming soon!)
  • Honest advice from real beginner experience
