Recommended Makeup Kit for Cosplay

Building your first cosplay makeup kit can be overwhelming. Here's a practical, beginner-friendly list of essential products that will cover most characters.

1. Skincare & Base

  • Light moisturizer / lotion
  • Primer (optional but recommended)
  • Cushion foundation or liquid foundation (2 shades)
  • Concealer (for blemishes and dark circles)
  • Setting powder / translucent powder

2. Eyes & Brows

  • Matte brown eyeshadow palette (for eye base & contour)
  • Eyeliner (black liquid or gel)
  • False eyelashes (cluster or individual)
  • Eyelid tape / glue
  • Eyebrow pencil or powder

3. Cheeks & Highlight

  • Blush (pink or peach shade)
  • Highlighter (cream or powder)
  • Contour powder (cool tone)

4. Lips & Tools

  • Nude / pink lipstick or lip tint
  • Lip liner
  • Makeup sponges
  • Makeup brushes set (especially small ones)
  • Setting spray

💡 Pro Tip: Start with affordable drugstore brands (e.g. Maybelline, Romand, Etude House) before investing in expensive ones.
