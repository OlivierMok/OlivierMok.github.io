🧴 Essential Cosplay Makeup Kit

For Beginner Cosplayers — Must-Have Products Recommended by a Cosplay Makeup Artist

💧 1. Hydration & Pre-Makeup Care

  • Hydrating spray (100ml size — easy to bring on metro)
  • Mineral water + stretchable wet compress cotton (for pre-makeup wet compress)
  • Vaseline + disposable lip brushes (thick layer to prevent dry lips)

🧴 2. Moisturizing & Priming

  • Glycerin (for oily/combination skin)
  • Paste cream / Moisturizing cream (for dry/mixed-dry skin)
  • Green primer (corrects redness)

✨ 3. Base Makeup

  • Concealer — Opera Phantom small sample or tube type (great for eyebrows)
  • Highlight liquid (light shade)
  • Foundation — Mistine (highly recommended)

🔒 4. Setting & Finishing

  • Berrime Setting Spray (super popular, “brainless choice”)
  • Loose powder (for setting)
  • Small powder cake (for final face highlight)
  • Gray-brown brow powder (avoid warm brown — looks dirty on pale cos skin)

🌸 5. Color Products

  • Blush — Peach color (very versatile for beginners)
  • Eyeshadow Palettes:
    • Orange Begonia palette (very versatile)
    • Peach palette (great for cute/male cos)
  • UCANBE Fruit Punch color palette (highly recommended)
  • Water-based face paint (for colorful designs)

💄 6. Lips & Eyes

  • 2 lipsticks (one earthy brown for male cos, one orange/pink for female cos)
  • False lashes — Small devil style (female), baby lashes (male)
  • Eyelash glue
  • Double eyelid tape (lace type, water-activated)
  • Eyebrow pencils (black + brown, fine tip)
  • Eyeliner pens (fine tip, black + brown)
  • Brow gel / Tint
  • Mascara (black)

🛠️ 7. Tools

  • Eyebrow razor
