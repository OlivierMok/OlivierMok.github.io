7 Practical Tips for Beginner Cosplay Makeup

  1. Start with Light Skincare (Pre-Makeup)
    Cosplay foundation is heavy, so begin with simple moisturizing. Apply lotion thinly on oily areas.
  2. Apply Foundation the Smart Way
    Apply against the direction of your pores. Use a beauty sponge and pat outward.
  3. Blend Thoroughly & Use Less Product
    Fold and roll your sponge to distribute product evenly and avoid cakey patches.
  4. Add Dimension with Contouring
    Use light and shadow to create a more three-dimensional face shape.
  5. Fix Puffy Eyes
    Use matte brown eyeshadow over the entire lid to reduce swelling.
  6. Make Your Eyes Pop
    Apply cluster lashes and stack eyelid tape if needed. Mark position under pupil first.
  7. Perfect Your Lips
    Draw a V at the cupid’s bow, extend upper lip into M shape, and highlight for plump lips.
